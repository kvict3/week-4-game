Welcome to CrystalGemsCollector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are three crystal gems below and Steven. By clicking on one of them, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number and you lose the game if your score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on each one.


Each time the game starts, the values of each character will change.


Good luck!
